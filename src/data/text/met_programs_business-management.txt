Prepare yourself to meet the evolving needs of industry with a specialized business and management program focusing on the latest competencies, technologies, and insights. Undergraduate or graduate, the degree and certificate programs in business and management at MET connect you to an engaged community and rigorous, relevant academics. Students gain first-hand business acumen from faculty with extensive industry and research experience, and make vital connections with peers from around the world. Through programs in applied business analytics, enterprise risk management, project management, supply chain management, among many more, MET provides an education that is practical, insightful, and geared toward adaptability in today’s rapidly evolving industry, conferring you with knowledge that can be applied on the job immediately.










#10, Best Online Master's in Business Programs (Excluding MBA)


MET’s online master’s degrees in management are ranked #10 in the nation by 
U.S. News & World Report
 for 2025.

Degrees




BS in Management Studies
Acquire an understanding of the concepts, theories, and applications of management and liberal arts, and develop critical thinking skills and analytical capabilities in this self-paced major.


Read More.






BS in Economics
Enhance your ability to analyze the challenges of business and society through modern quantitative methods and training in economic theory and institutions, opening the door to government, legal, business, and teaching professions.


Read More.






MS in Actuarial Science
Gain the skills to become a successful actuary through specialized courses in statistics, mathematics, economics, finance, data science, and related areas.


Read More.






MS in Actuarial Science, Finance Concentration
Benefit from in-depth knowledge of investment strategies, asset-liability management, and financial modeling techniques specific to insurance and pension fund operations.


Read More.






MS in Actuarial Science, Insurance Concentration
Develop expertise in product development, underwriting, and claims management through a curriculum emphasizing risk assessment, policy design, and pricing strategies.


Read More.






MS in Administrative Studies, Innovation & Technology Concentration
This MSAS concentration in Innovation & Technology focuses on developing innovation and technology strategies inorder to transform a business model and provide new revenue and value-producing opportunities.


Read More.






MS in Advertising
Combine the study of core advertising fundamentals with the latest interactive multimedia and web marketing techniques.


Read More.






MS in Applied Business Analytics
Gain hands-on experience with a variety of analytical models and decision-support tools that you can apply to interlinked data-inputs and large data sets in the areas of marketing, operations, product and technology innovations, financial services, and others.


Read More.






MS in Construction Management & Technology
Prepare for the evolving needs of the built environment and construction sector with a graduate degree that integrates project management fundamentals with Construction 5.0 technologies—including AI analytics, BIM, digital twins, drones, and robotics—and aligns with PMI Construction Professional (PMI-CP)™ certification.


Read More.






MS in Enterprise Risk Management
Master the essential tools and theories needed to lead change, think strategically in complex business situations, and provide analytical and technological solutions that mitigate risk across entire enterprises—knowledge you can immediately apply in an exciting, data-driven industry.


Read More.






MS in Financial Management
Prepare for the highly competitive global financial services environment by developing career-building skills in corporate finance, financial management, investment analysis, and multinational finance.


Read More.






MS in Financial Management, AI Applications Concentration
The MSFM concentration in Artificial Intelligence Applications prepares you for roles in financial technology, algorithmic trading, quantitative analysis, and risk management by focusing on AI-driven financial modeling, data analytics, and machine learning techniques applied to finance.


Read More.






MS in Financial Management, International Finance Concentration
The MSFM concentration in International Finance examines prepares you to excel at credit risk evaluation, quantitative security selection, risk measurement and mitigation, cash management, and understanding the impact of foreign exchange rates on cost of capital and budgeting.


Read More.






MS in Financial Management, Investment Analysis Concentration
The MSFM concentration in Investment Analysis examines investment strategies, asset valuation, and investment management, preparing you for a variety of careers in finance involving analysis and valuation of financial assets, as well as risk management.


Read More.






MS in Financial Management, Sustainability Concentration
The MSFM concentration in Sustainability builds the skills needed to integrate environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors into financial decision-making, opening the door to roles in sustainable finance, socially responsible investing, corporate sustainability, and risk management.


Read More.






MS in Global Marketing Management
Enhance your understanding of international business, economics, market research, and culture while developing skills in ecommerce, digital marketing, market research and data analysis, finance, project management, and product and service development.


Read More.






MS in Innovation & Entrepreneurship
Gain the strategic, analytical, and entrepreneurial skills to lead innovation and drive transformation across startups and established enterprises in today’s technology-driven global economy.


Read More.






MS in Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Strategy Concentration
The MSIE concentration in Strategy nurtures the strategic insight and analytical tools to evaluate opportunities, manage risk, and drive competitive growth and innovation across diverse global markets.


Read More.






MS in Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Sustainability Concentration
The MSIE concentration in Sustainability empowers you to create innovative, impact-driven ventures that align financial performance with environmental responsibility, social equity, and ethical leadership in a rapidly evolving global economy.


Read More.






MS in Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Technology Concentration
The MSIE concentration in Technology equips you with technical, analytical, and entrepreneurial skills to harness data, emerging technologies, and digital tools to launch, grow, and innovate tech-driven ventures.


Read More.






MS in Insurance Management
Complement your insurance expertise with an understanding of general management principles in accounting, finance, operations, and leadership—part of a curriculum designed specifically for professionals who have acquired the Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter (CPCU) Credential.


Read More.






MS in Project Management
Learn to manage complex projects, facilitate communication between the project team and clients, and balance competing demands among scope, time, cost, and quality. Accredited by the PMI® Global Accreditation Center for Project Management Education Programs (GAC).


Read More.






MS in Project Management, Program & Project Portfolio Management Concentration
The MSPM concentration in Program & Portfolio Management provides an expanded enterprise view and hands on practice in the collection of programs and portfolios.


Read More.






MS in Supply Chain Management
Examine the design, optimization, and operation of a global supply chain and learn to analyze and improve business process flows and become a confident decision-maker. Optional concentrations include Analytics, Logistics Management, and Risk Management.


Read More.






MS in Supply Chain Management, Analytics Concentration
The MSSCM concentration in Analytics develops expertise in operations-related analytics, including supply chain strategies, analysis and mitigation of supply chain risks, management of performance, quality control, and inventory and capacity management.


Read More.






MS in Supply Chain Management, Logistics Management Concentration
The MSSCM concentration in Logistics Management emphasizes decision-making in the areas of transportation management, warehouse and distribution center operations, and logistics network design—including an understanding of key tradeoffs and the effect of variance in a logistics system.


Read More.






MS in Supply Chain Management, Risk Management Concentration
The MSSCM concentration in Risk Management examines business continuity planning and crisis management, including the skills needed to identify emergent threats and develop strategic responses that adhere to proven enterprise risk management principles.


Read More.

Financial Management Graduate Certificate

Global Marketing Management Graduate Certificate
Learn internet-based marketing skills such as ecommerce principles, web design, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), and Search Engine Marketing (SEM), and develop an understanding of social media and platform business models in today’s highly competitive and international environment.


Read More.






Innovation & Entrepreneurship Graduate Certificate
Learn the key managerial competencies required to facilitate the development of commercialized innovations and bring new products and solutions to a pre-commercial stage relevant to rapidly changing technological, economic, and cultural environments.


Read More.






IT Project Management Graduate Certificate
Master IT project management tools and techniques, and gain expertise in agile software development practices and software risk management, cost estimation, and quality management.

International Business Management Graduate Certificate

Read More.






International Business Management Undergraduate Certificate
Learn how businesses and corporations operate in global markets, gain international perspectives of industry, and refine skills you can apply to your career.


Read More.






International Finance Graduate Certificate
Specialize in the highly desired area of international finance and develop the knowledge to address corporate questions pertaining to foreign exchange risk, transaction and translation exposure, and corporate strategy and foreign direct investment.


Read More.






Investment Analysis Graduate Certificate
Develop in-depth knowledge of investment strategies, asset valuation, and investment management that can be applied to a variety of careers in finance involving analysis and valuation of financial assets as well as risk management.


Read More.






Project Management Graduate Certificate
Conduct project simulations, examine detailed case studies, and gain both theoretical and practical expertise in project management tools and techniques, with an emphasis on communications, risk analysis, cost estimation and budgeting, and quality control.


Read More.






Project, Program & Portfolio Management Graduate Certificate
Learn the principles, practices, and activities of program and portfolio management that support good practices across all industry domains.


Read More.






Supply Chain Management Graduate Certificate
Learn key operations management concepts, methods, and the state-of-the-art tools used in the process of designing and managing global supply chains.


Read More.
