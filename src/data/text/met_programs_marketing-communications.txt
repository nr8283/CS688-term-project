Marketing & Communications Programs


















Degrees & Certificates
News & Events



Practical, cutting edge, and well-rounded, the programs in marketing and communications at BU’s Metropolitan College (MET) combine core fundamentals in advertising and messaging strategy with the latest approaches in digital content marketing, social media, public relations, and targeted marketing.


Through the hands-on, professional marketing master’s degree and graduate certificate programs, students gain understandings of interactive and digital content, whether focusing on advertising, international marketing, or health communication management.


Students are prepared to compete in the fast-paced, ever-changing world of communication—whether they are entering a new field, like health marketing, or enhancing their relevant industry knowledge and experience in traditional and global arenas. MET’s programs in advertising and health communications are each offered in conjunction with BU’s College of Communication. The Global Marketing Management (MSGMM) program at BU MET pairs a solid academic core in marketing with relevant classes in e-commerce, financial and economic analysis, project management, product and service development, and data analysis.


Faculty offer lessons to master the arts of pitching, capitalizing on the new tools in emerging media, and working collaboratively—skills that give you an edge in the daily challenges of any job. Learn to solve contemporary marketing problems from strategic, creative, and technological perspectives, across platforms and arenas, with a graduate program in marketing and communications at BU MET.

Degrees




MS in Advertising
Combine the study of core advertising fundamentals with the latest interactive multimedia and web marketing techniques.


Read More.






MS in Global Marketing Management
Enhance your understanding of international business, economics, market research, and culture while developing skills in ecommerce, digital marketing, market research and data analysis, finance, project management, and product and service development.


Read More.






MS in Health Communication
Focus on the intersection of healthcare, marketing, health literacy, public relations, and digital media while learning how to develop effective communication campaigns, tackle tough healthcare issues, and provide valuable information to the media, the public, and other critical stakeholders.


Read More.






MS in Health Communication, Healthcare Promotion, Media & Marketing Concentration
The MSHC concentration in Healthcare Promotion, Media & Marketing develops the specific skills needed to create effective, integrated public relations, media, and marketing plans that help achieve the goals of health-related organizations and clients.


Read More.






MS in Health Communication, Visual & Digital Health Communication Concentration
The MSHC concentration in Visual & Digital Health Communication focuses on the effective utilization of powerful health education and delivery application tools, such as infographics, data visualization graphics, videos, websites, mobile apps, and branded content.


Read More.








Certificates




Arts Marketing Graduate Certificate
Develop a sophisticated skillset in the latest marketing and advertising approaches and prepare for a role as a marketing manager within an arts organization or program.  


Read More.






Foundations of Health Communication Graduate Certificate
Explore the foundational health communication principles, including awareness of health systems and stakeholders, population-based research, campaign planning, and using health literacy strategies and plain language for diverse populations.


Read More.






Global Marketing Management Graduate Certificate
Learn internet-based marketing skills such as ecommerce principles, web design, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), and Search Engine Marketing (SEM), and develop an understanding of social media and platform business models in today’s highly competitive and international environment.


Read More.






Healthcare Promotion, Media & Marketing Graduate Certificate
Gain workplace-ready skills while learning how to frame points of view and create powerful messages using print, broadcast, social, community, and organizational media in creative ways to influence and educate diverse audiences.


Read More.






Innovation & Entrepreneurship Graduate Certificate
Learn the key managerial competencies required to facilitate the development of commercialized innovations and bring new products and solutions to a pre-commercial stage relevant to rapidly changing technological, economic, and cultural environments.


Read More.






Visual & Digital Health Communication Graduate Certificate
Study the fundamental principles of design and how to apply them to the creation of powerful health education and delivery application tools, such as infographics, data visualization graphics, videos, websites, mobile apps, and branded content.


Read More.
