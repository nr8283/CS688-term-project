Connect With An Ambassador






Ready to learn more about the graduate student experience at BU Metropolitan College? Do you have admissions questions? Connect directly with one of our BU MET Student or Alum Ambassadors or Graduate Admissions Advisors via Unibuddy Chat.


Ask a Student or Alum Ambassador


If you have questions about what it is like to be a graduate student at MET, connect with a Student or Alum Ambassador. Our ambassadors are from all over the world, representing our various degree and certificate programs. Start a conversation:




Select the “Students” tab below and choose an ambassador to chat with.


Create your Unibuddy account and complete your quick profile.


Adjust your communication preferences under “settings.”




Ask an Admissions Advisor


For questions concerning admissions, applications, tuition, or financial aid, please speak to a Graduate Admissions Advisor:




Select the “Staff” tab below and choose an advisor to chat with.


Create your Unibuddy account and complete your quick profile.


Adjust your communication preferences under “settings.”




Our Ambassadors and Graduate Admissions Advisors will answer your questions within the next 24 to 48 hours. For other questions, or if you have issues accessing the Unibuddy platform, please reach out to Admissions at 
met@bu.edu
.
















Contact an Admissions Advisor


We recommend speaking to an admissions advisor before you get started. You can discuss the right course of study for your needs, learn more about registering for courses, or determine what documents you may need to apply to a program at MET. Use the button below to request information and an advisor will be in touch.
