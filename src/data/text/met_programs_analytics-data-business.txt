Analytics, Data & Business Programs

Faculty
News & Events



Given the tremendous growth of digital data—and the desire of enterprises to derive more and more value from it—data analytics talent is in high demand worldwide. The big data boom reverberates through every aspect of modern life, driving new technology while requiring business and tech leaders to become experts capable of adjusting to the rapidly evolving opportunities related to data and its analysis. To help bridge this skills gap and to ready students for burgeoning data scientist jobs and data analyst positions, BU’s Metropolitan College (MET) offers a range of graduate programs in applied data and business analytics.

Degrees




MS in Applied Business Analytics
Gain hands-on experience with a variety of analytical models and decision-support tools that you can apply to interlinked data-inputs and large data sets in the areas of marketing, operations, product and technology innovations, financial services, and others.


Read More.






MS in Applied Data Analytics
Learn to create powerful predictions through modeling and machine learning, and drive critical business decisions using data mining and visualization tools, Python packages, R packages, and a variety of cloud services.


Read More.






MS in Computer Information Systems, Data Analytics Concentration
The MSCIS concentration in Data Analytics develops the data processing, analysis, and visualization skills required to compete for data analytics jobs amid rising global demand.


Read More.






MS in Computer Science, Data Analytics Concentration
The MSCS concentration in Data Analytics is a career-centric program offering extensive exposure to database systems, data mining tools, data visualization tools, and cloud services. 


Read More.






MS in Health Informatics, Applied Data Analytics Concentration
The Master of Science in Health Informatics concentration in Applied Data Analytics focuses on developing sought-after skills in healthcare data processing, analysis, and visualization.


Read More.






MS in Supply Chain Management, Analytics Concentration
The MSSCM concentration in Analytics develops expertise in operations-related analytics, including supply chain strategies, analysis and mitigation of supply chain risks, management of performance, quality control, and inventory and capacity management.


Read More.








Certificates




Data Analytics Graduate Certificate
Explore data analytics and various topics related to data processing, analysis, and visualization that will prepare you to take on data analyst or data scientist roles, among others. 


Read More.






Applied Business Analytics Graduate Certificate
Immerse yourself in comprehensive coverage of data analytics concepts, techniques, and state-of-the-art tools used in the process of data-driven business decision-making.


Read More.

Pre-Analytics Laboratory AD 100
