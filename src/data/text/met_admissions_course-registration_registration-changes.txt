Registration Changes














Deferral of Admission


Admitted students may defer their admission for up to one full year from their term of admission. To defer, admitted students must complete an 
Enrollment Status Change Request
 before the first day of the term of admission.


International students who defer will be required to submit updated international student documentation (including financial support). Students will be notified by BU Metropolitan College when this documentation becomes necessary.




Dropping a Course


To drop a course or courses, visit the 
MyBU Student Portal
. If you are unable to drop your course on the MyBU Student Portal, you must submit the 
Add/Drop Form
. 


Students dropping to zero courses in a semester must complete an 
Enrollment Status Change Request
 to be dropped from their course(s). 


Upon receipt of the appropriate form, your account will be adjusted according to the 
University’s refund policy
. 
Please be aware that the add/drop deadlines as noted on MET’s 
academic calendars
 may differ from the refund dates
.




Withdrawing From All Courses


To withdraw from all of your courses, you must complete the 
Enrollment Status Change Request
. 


Upon receipt of this form, your account will be adjusted according to the 
University’s refund policy
. Withdrawal deadlines can be found on the 
academic calendars
. Please be aware that add/drop and withdrawal deadlines may be different than the refund dates.


Financial Aid Recipients


The policies above are academic and related only to tuition refunds. Separate regulations govern federal student loans and may require BU to reduce or cancel a previously disbursed loan when your enrollment changes.


Learn how dropping a course, withdrawing from the semester, or taking a leave of absence may affect your eligibility for financial aid or scholarships:




Graduate students should contact 
finanaid@bu.edu


Undergraduate students should contact 
finaid@bu.edu
 or 617-358-2792
