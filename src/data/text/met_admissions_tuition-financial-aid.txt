Metropolitan College Tuition Rates


In general, Metropolitan College tuition is set according to the number of units being taken, plus any additional fees or course costs. The tuition rates listed below are applicable to courses and programs on campus and online. Note that tuition is different for MET courses taken on campus during 
Summer Term.


Please note:
 
Metropolitan College students who register for courses offered by other Boston University schools and colleges will be charged the 
tuition and fees of that school or college
.

Student Health Insurance Plan (SHIP)


Massachusetts law requires all students carrying at least 75 percent of the full-time curriculum to be covered under a qualifying medical insurance plan. If you do not wish to participate in the Boston University Student Health Insurance Plan, you have the opportunity to waive SHIP coverage—provided you have other coverage that meets the comparable coverage requirements. For more information, see the 
Student Accounting Services Student Health Insurance page
.
