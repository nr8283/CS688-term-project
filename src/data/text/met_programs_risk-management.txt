Risk Management Programs

Faculty
News & Events



In a dynamic global arena, organizations need to be prepared for unforeseen developments and their potential cost ramifications. These manifold risks to the financial well-being of companies and businesses creates need for educated risk managers prepared to guide organizations towards best practices. The programs in risk management at Boston University’s Metropolitan College (MET) integrate cutting-edge academic learnings with the proven management practices adhered to by leading global corporations.


The imperative of the 21st century business environment, enterprise risk management (ERM) is a holistic approach to identifying potential threats, risks, and vulnerabilities in organizations and business enterprise. The ability to contribute ongoing strategies to evaluate and mitigate potential disruptions—including resiliency planning, prevention, crisis management, business continuity management, and recovery— encompass vital skill sets for leadership in business and all enterprises.


Whether through the Enterprise Risk Management master’s degree program or the four-course graduate certificate, BU MET offers sequences of courses that prepare motivated professionals to tackle enterprise risk challenges in today’s turbulent business world, enhancing value by providing solutions that mitigate risk across entire business enterprises and efforts.

Degrees




MS in Enterprise Risk Management
Master the essential tools and theories needed to lead change, think strategically in complex business situations, and provide analytical and technological solutions that mitigate risk across entire enterprises—knowledge you can immediately apply in an exciting, data-driven industry.


Read More.






MS in Insurance Management
Complement your insurance expertise with an understanding of general management principles in accounting, finance, operations, and leadership—part of a curriculum designed specifically for professionals who have acquired the Chartered Property Casualty Underwriter (CPCU) Credential.


Read More.






MS in Supply Chain Management, Risk Management Concentration
The MSSCM concentration in Risk Management examines business continuity planning and crisis management, including the skills needed to identify emergent threats and develop strategic responses that adhere to proven enterprise risk management principles.


Read More.








Certificates




Enterprise Risk Management Graduate Certificate
Gain expertise in the proactive aspects of enterprise risk management, examine a range of technical and logistical issues, and learn to formulate and execute plans, frameworks, and processes for business continuity and disaster recovery.
